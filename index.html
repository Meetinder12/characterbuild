Monkey D Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy made his debut as a young boy who acquires the properties of rubber after accidentally eating one of the Devil Fruits that belonged to "Red Hair" Shanks. He is the captain of the Straw Hats who dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates and finding One Piece. The Devil Fruit he ate was the Gomu Gomu no devil fruit, which turned his body into rubber.

Roronova Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is a main combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates, one of their two swordsmen, one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, and is publicly recognized as the right-hand man of his crew's captain Monkey D. Luffy.

Sanji

Sanji is a cook and a fighter in the Straw Hat Pirates, a group of adventurers in the manga and anime series One Piece. He joined the crew in the East Blue, where he worked at an ocean-going restaurant called Baratie. He has a secret history as a member of the Vinsmoke Family, a powerful and ruthless clan of assassins. He is known for his Black-leg Style, a martial art that uses only his legs, and his love for women.

Nico Robin

Nico Robin is a character in the One Piece series who first appeared in the 114th chapter of the series. She is introduced as an antagonist, but eventually becomes the seventh member of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, captained by Monkey D. Luffy. Robin is an archaeologist who is well-versed in information and affairs, and can produce “Hanahana-no-Fruit,” a devil fruit that can make any part of her body bloom at will. She is known as the 'Devil Child' due to the fact that her mother researched the Blank Era.
